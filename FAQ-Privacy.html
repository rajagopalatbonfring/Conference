Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions & Answers

General Questions

What is ICAC 2025? ▼
ICAC 2025 is the International Conference on Advanced Computing & Communication, a premier conference bringing together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders to share cutting-edge research in computing technologies.
When and where is the conference? ▼
ICAC 2025 will be held September 15-17, 2025, at the Grand Convention Center in New Delhi, India. Both in-person and virtual attendance options are available.
Who should attend? ▼
ICAC welcomes researchers, students, academics, industry professionals, and anyone interested in advanced computing and communication technologies. All experience levels are welcome.

Paper Submission Questions

What are the paper submission deadlines? ▼
Abstract deadline is January 31, 2025. Full paper submission deadline is March 31, 2025. Accepted authors will be notified by May 15, 2025.
What are the paper formatting requirements? ▼
Papers should be 8 pages (content) + 2 pages (references), single-spaced, 12pt Times New Roman, 1-inch margins. Use provided templates in LaTeX or MS Word format.
Can I submit to multiple conferences simultaneously? ▼
No, papers must be original and not under review elsewhere. Simultaneous submissions to other conferences are not permitted.
How long does the review process take? ▼
The peer review process takes approximately 6 weeks. Authors will receive decision notifications and reviewer comments by May 15, 2025.

Registration Questions

What is the registration fee? ▼
Early bird rates (until July 1): Students $199, Professionals $399. Regular rates (until Aug 15): Students $249, Professionals $499. Late rates available until Sept 10.
What does registration include? ▼
Registration includes conference access (all 3 days), keynote sessions, research presentations, meals (breakfast, lunch, coffee breaks), conference materials, and certificate of attendance.
Is there a group discount? ▼
Yes! Groups of 5 or more get 15% discount. For institutional/group pricing, please contact register@icac2025.org.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Full refund if cancelled before July 1. 50% refund if cancelled before August 15. No refunds after August 15, 2025.

Venue & Logistics

How do I reach the venue? ▼
The venue is easily accessible by air (22 km from airport), train (8 km), and road. Free parking available. Check the Venue page for detailed travel information.
Are hotels available? ▼
Yes, several partner hotels available with group discounts: The Grand Plaza (0.5 km), Tech City Hotel (1.2 km), and Budget Inn (2 km). See Venue page for booking codes.
Do I need a visa? ▼
Visa requirements depend on your country. Check with Indian embassy. Business/Conference visas are typically available. We can provide invitation letters for visa applications.

Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how International Conference on Advanced Computing & Communication (ICAC 2025) collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand our practices.

Last Updated: December 2025

Information We Collect

During Registration:

  • Full name and contact information (email, phone)
  • Institutional affiliation
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Dietary preferences and accessibility requirements

During Paper Submission:

  • Author names and affiliations
  • Paper content and metadata
  • Research keywords and topics

Automatically Collected:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Cookies and tracking data

How We Use Your Information

  • Process conference registration and payment
  • Send updates about conference dates and schedule
  • Facilitate peer review process for papers
  • Publish accepted papers in conference proceedings
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, secure payment processing, and restricted access to personal data. We do not sell or share your information with third parties without consent, except as required by law.

Your Rights

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to correct or update your information
  • Right to delete your data (upon request)
  • Right to opt-out of communications

Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact: privacy@icac2025.org
