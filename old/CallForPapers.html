Formatting Requirements

  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt
  • Spacing: Single-spaced
  • Margins: 1 inch (2.54 cm) on all sides
  • Page Limit: 8 pages content + 2 pages references
  • Title: Bold, 14pt, centered
  • Headings: Numbered sections with hierarchical formatting
  • Figures/Tables: Include captions, reference in text
  • References: IEEE or ACM style, alphabetically sorted

Peer Review Process

Phase 1: Abstract Screening (2 weeks)

  • Editors review abstracts for scope alignment
  • Plagiarism check (threshold: 15%)
  • Quality & relevance assessment
  • Status: Accepted / Request Revision / Reject

Phase 2: Peer Review (6 weeks)

  • Minimum 2 expert reviewers assigned
  • Blind review process (double-blind)
  • Reviewers evaluate: originality, quality, relevance, clarity
  • Confidential comments provided to authors
  • Recommendations: Accept / Minor Revision / Major Revision / Reject

Phase 3: Author Revision (Optional)

  • If revisions requested, authors have 3 weeks
  • Submit revised paper + response letter
  • Reviewers assess revisions
  • Final decision: Accept / Reject

Phase 4: Production

  • Accepted papers move to production
  • Authors submit camera-ready version
  • Copyediting and formatting final check
  • Papers published in proceedings

Important Guidelines

‚úì Plagiarism Policy

All papers are scanned for plagiarism using Turnitin. Similarity index must be < 15% (proper citations excluded). Papers with > 25% similarity will be rejected.

‚ö†Ô∏è Conflict of Interest

Authors must disclose potential conflicts of interest at submission. Reviewers with conflicts will be recused.

‚úã Ethical Guidelines

Papers must comply with research ethics standards. Research involving human subjects requires IRB approval. All co-authors must be listed and agree with submission.

üë• Co-authored Papers

All co-authors must be listed on submission. Only the corresponding author can make changes post-submission. Include emails and affiliations for all authors.

üîÑ Double-Blind Review

ICAC 2025 uses double-blind peer review. Ensure author names/affiliations are NOT visible in the manuscript. Use anonymous references (e.g., "Author et al." instead of your name).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I submit multiple papers?

Yes, you can submit up to 3 papers per author. However, at most 2 papers per author will be accepted for the conference.

What is the plagiarism threshold?

Papers with similarity index 0-15%: Accepted. 15-25%: Manual review. >25%: Likely rejection.

Can I submit already-published work?

No, papers must be original and unpublished. Previously published work or simultaneous submissions to other venues are not allowed.

What if my paper is rejected?

You receive detailed reviewer feedback. You can address comments and resubmit to next year's conference or a different venue.

Is there an open access fee?

No, publication is fully open access. All accepted papers are freely available online. Authors don't pay publication fees.

Can I withdraw my paper?

Yes, you can withdraw anytime before final decision is made. Withdrawals after acceptance incur a small administrative fee.

How long does the review process take?

Abstract review: 2 weeks. Full paper review: 6 weeks. Revisions: 3 weeks. Total: approximately 11 weeks from submission to decision.

Will I get reviewer feedback?

Yes, all authors receive detailed reviewer comments and suggestions for improvement, regardless of acceptance decision.
