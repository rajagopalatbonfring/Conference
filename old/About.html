Mission & Vision

Our Mission

ICAC 2025 is dedicated to bringing together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders from around the world to share cutting-edge research, innovations, and best practices in advanced computing and communication technologies. We provide a premier platform for knowledge exchange and networking.

Our Vision

To foster a global community of computing and communication professionals committed to advancing the field through research, education, and collaborative innovation. We envision a world where technological breakthroughs improve human lives and solve critical global challenges.

Conference History

2013 - The Beginning
ICAC was first established as a regional workshop with 50 participants. It focused on local computing research trends and industry insights.
2015 - First National Conference
Expanded to national level with 150 participants and 30 research papers. Included keynote speakers from leading institutions.
2018 - International Recognition
Became an international conference with participants from 12 countries, 250+ attendees, and papers indexed in Scopus and Google Scholar.
2020 - Virtual Adaptation
Successfully transitioned to hybrid format during global pandemic, reaching 400+ attendees from 25 countries worldwide.
2023 - Record Breaking
ICAC 2023 achieved highest attendance with 450 participants, 180 paper submissions, and partnerships with 20 international institutions.
2025 - New Heights
ICAC 2025 aims to surpass previous records with expanded research tracks, more keynote speakers, and enhanced networking opportunities.
