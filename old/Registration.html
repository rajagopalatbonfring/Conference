Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely.

Can I get a refund?

Full refund if cancelled before July 1, 2025. 50% refund if cancelled before August 15, 2025. No refunds after August 15.

Is accommodation included?

No, accommodation is separate. We provide discounted rates at partner hotels. Check venue page for details.

Can I transfer my registration?

Yes, you can transfer your registration to another person free of charge until August 31, 2025.

Is there a group discount?

Yes! Groups of 5+ get 15% discount. Contact us for institutional/group pricing.

Do students need valid ID?

Yes, students must show valid student ID at registration. Professional rates apply if ID cannot be verified.
