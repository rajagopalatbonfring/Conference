Important Reminders

  • No Extensions: All submission deadlines are strict. No extensions will be granted.
  • Timezone: All deadlines are in UTC (Coordinated Universal Time)
  • Plagiarism Check: All papers are checked for plagiarism. Similarity index must be < 15%
  • Blind Review: Do NOT include author information in submitted papers
  • At Least One Author: At least one author must register and present the paper
  • Paper Format: Follow provided templates (LaTeX or Word)
